Shiro Wat

Description

Shiro Wat is a popular Ethiopian vegetarian stew made from ground chickpeas and spices. It is affordable, nutritious, and enjoyed throughout Ethiopia.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup shiro powder
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 3 cups water
  • 1 tablespoon berbere spice
  • Salt to taste

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a pot and sauté the onion until golden.
  2. Add garlic and berbere spice.
  3. Pour in water and bring to a gentle boil.
  4. Pour in water and bring to a gentle boil.
  5. Cook for 15–20 minutes until thick and smooth.
  6. Add salt to taste.
  7. Serve hot with Injera.
