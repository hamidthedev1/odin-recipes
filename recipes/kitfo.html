Kitfo

Description

Kitfo is a traditional Ethiopian dish made from finely minced beef mixed with spiced butter and mitmita. It is often served with ayib (Ethiopian cottage cheese) and gomen (collard greens).

Ingredients

  • 500 g lean beef, finely minced
  • 3 tablespoons niter kibbeh
  • 1 tablespoon mitmita spice
  • Salt to taste
  • Ayib (optional)
  • Gomen (optional)

Steps

  1. Melt the niter kibbeh in a pan over low heat.
  2. Add mitmita and mix well.
  3. Add the minced beef and stir gently.
  4. Cook lightly for medium-rare, or longer if preferred.
  5. Add salt to taste.
  6. Serve with ayib, gomen, and Injera.
